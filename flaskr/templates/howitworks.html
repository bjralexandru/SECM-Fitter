What is SECM Fitter?

SF is intended to be a one stop service for your Scanning Electrochemical Microscopy (SECM) approach curve fitting. It uses Cornut, et. al's fitting equation in order to extract the heterogeneous rate constant from your approach curve experiments. Additionally, it should exempt you from having multiple excel sheets, each one with a different experiment, because you now can query all your past fittings and look-up for the data you need.

My hope is that this project will turn into an open-source database containing as much data as possible about SECM experiments, and this will lead to an increase in transparency and reproducibility in future studies.
